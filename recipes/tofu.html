Black Pepper Tofu

A good recipe to make for people that say tofu can never taste like anything. This is actually peak food and so tasteful.

Inspired by Yotam Ottolenghi's recipe.

Ingredients

  • 800g firm tofu
  • Cornflour, to dust the tofu
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 150g vegan butter
  • 12 small shallots (350g), peeled and thinly sliced
  • 8 red chillies, thinly sliced
  • 12 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 3 tablespoons chopped ginger
  • 5 tablespoons crushed black peppercorns
  • 10 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Steps

  1. Pour enough oil into a large frying pan so that it can come up 5mm on the sides of the pan.
  2. Cut tofu in medium to large cubes and toss them in cornflour. Shake off excess and fry the tofu, turning them, ensuring the pieces are golden all around and have a thin crust.
  3. Remove the oil and clean the pan, add the butter and melt it.
  4. Add shallots, chillies, garlic, ginger and saut√© on low to medium heat for about 15 minutes.
  5. Once the ingredients are shiny and soft add the soy sauce and sugar and stir, then add the black pepper.
  6. Add the tofu to warm it up in the sauce for about a minute.
