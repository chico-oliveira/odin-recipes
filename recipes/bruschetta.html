Bruschetta

A wonderful and light Bruschetta recipe for when you're running out of time and need to prep something quickly so your partner doesn't get mad at you.

Inspired by me :)

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 to 8 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 large Mozarella balls
  • Salt, pepper and chilli flakes to taste
  • 1 loaf bread

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven at 180ÂºC
  2. In a bowl, mix tomatoes, garlic and basil, taking care not to mash or break up the cherry tomatoes too much. Add the balsamic vinegar and extra virgin olive oil. Add salt and pepper to taste. Mix again.
  3. Slice the loaf diagonally into 12 thick slices and place them on a tray. Drizzle with olive oil and add mixture on top of the slices.
  4. Slice the Mozarella and place on top of the slices. Sprinkle pepper and chilli flakes on the cheese to taste.
  5. Place tray in oven and remove once the slices toast and cheese melts slightly
